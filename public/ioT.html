Internet of Things(IoT)

Training Session : 21 feb - 25 feb

Timings : 5:30 pm - 7:30 pm


About Speaker : Mr Saket Kumar

Saket Kumar presently working as Head, Innovation and IPR in Amity Innovation and Design Center, Amity School of Engineering & Technology, Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Noida, UP, INDIA, (Graded by NAAC and ranked 32 in NIRF) from Aug, 2017 to till date. Additionally, he has more than nine-year teaching experience at UG and PG level in various engineering Institutes/University. He has completed his Post Graduate degree at Mewar University, Chittorgarh, Rajasthan, India in area of VLSI in 2016.


Topics that will be covered :

  • Day 1: Introduction to Electronics and Arduino Programming
  • Day 2: Controlling Sensors and Actuators with Arduino
  • Day 3: Sending Sensor Data to ThingSpeak/Udibots Cloud, Communication with RF Transmitters
  • Day 4: Making a BLE controlled bot
  • Day 5: Getting started with Raspberry Pi
